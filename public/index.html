The one rule

Freediving has a single, non-negotiable rule — never dive alone. Yet most of us, most days, have no one to dive with.

Surface exists to solve exactly that. Find a buddy who matches your level and goals, agree on a plan, and keep each other safe — in the water and out.

Three disciplines

Log every kind of dive

Static, dynamic and depth — each tracked in its own units, each with your personal best surfaced automatically on your profile.

Static apnea

A motionless breath-hold. Measured in time — the long, quiet discipline of patience.

Measured in mm:ss

Dynamic apnea

Horizontal distance on a single breath, usually in a pool. Measured in metres.

Measured in metres

Depth

A vertical descent along a line into open water. Measured by how deep you go.

Measured in metres
